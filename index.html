The Weeknd has just announced dates for The After Hours tour. Named after his upcoming album–which is due out on March 20–fans all throughout North America, the UK and Europe will have a chance to catch the performer, whose real name is Abel Tesfaye, live.

The tour kicks off with two nights at the Rogers Arena in Vancouver starting on June 11. He’ll be joined by Sabrina Claudio and Don Toliver for the duration of the trek, which wraps up its North American leg on September 3 in New York, NY at Madison Square Garden. Along the way, catch him in Detroit, Boston, New Orleans, Portland, Salt Lake City, Tulsa, Los Angeles and a whole lot more. Check out the full concert schedule below + ticket onsale info.
